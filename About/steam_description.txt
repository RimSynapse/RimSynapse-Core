[h1]RimSynapse - Core[/h1]
[b]The foundational Large Language Model (LLM) connector and narrative intelligence engine for RimWorld.[/b]

RimSynapse Core is a powerful, asynchronous background engine that natively connects RimWorld to local Large Language Models (LLMs). It acts as the central brain of the RimSynapse ecosystem, automatically intercepting vanilla game events and orchestrating them into rich, cohesive story arcs.

[h2]Key Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]LLM Direct Action Console (Command in Plain English!):[/b] Control the game world using natural language! Open the Direct Action Console, type what you want in plain English (e.g. [i]"Make Fred have 20 Shooting, remove the Bipolar trait from Sarah, and start a fire on the solar generator"[/i]), and watch the Storyteller LLM act as an active agent, translating your request into native in-game tool calls and executing them in real-time!
[*] [b]Universal Backend Support:[/b] Connects natively to LM Studio, Ollama, or any OpenAI-compatible local API (e.g. OpenRouter, LocalAI) without complex setups.
[*] [b]Background Threading:[/b] Never drops a frame. All LLM requests are queued, processed, and sanitized asynchronously on separate threads.
[*] [b]Dynamic Priority Queue:[/b] Intelligently manages hundreds of requests with an advanced Linux-style top-monitor queue system, prioritizing critical events and starving obsolete prompts.
[*] [b]Auto-Discovery & Keep-Alive:[/b] Automatically detects active models and manages keep-alive pings to ensure your LLMs don't go to sleep.
[*] [b]Event Tracking Ledger:[/b] Snapshots colony wealth, nutrition, and colonist status chronologically when events trigger, feeding the AI perfect narrative context.
[*] [b]Faction Perception Tracking:[/b] Factions dynamically adjust their subjective perception of your wealth and strength through visits and trade.
[*] [b]Colony Snapshots:[/b] Captures snapshots of the colony's mood, health, and stats when events occur to provide rich context.
[*] [b]Memory Infrastructure:[/b] Provides the per-pawn memory bank, weighted tag system, and decay engine used by companion mods to give colonists persistent, evolving memories.
[*] [b]Opportunistic Task Scheduling:[/b] A background task manager that feeds low-priority LLM requests (backstory generation, memory creation) into idle GPU cycles without impacting gameplay performance.
[/list]
